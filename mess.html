BMS Hostel

MESS

A vegetarian mess is being run on self-service and dividing system. Every student of the MSRIT is automatically a boarder at the time of admission to the hostel. Every boarder shall see to it that, congenial atmosphere is maintained in the dining hall to enable the staff to do their job effectively. No boarder shall enter into a quarrel or unnecessary discussion with staff or co-boarders. Outside food is not allowed into the dining hall/hostel premises except purchased from the stores situated within the premises. Service in the mess shall be as per the scheduled timings only. There shall be no service to the rooms and food will not be preserved for late comers.
